Redesign Plan: SUSANTEDIT Elite Gaming Platform
This plan outlines the complete transformation of the SUSANTEDIT website into a "1st place" worthy, premium, and immersive experience. We will integrate 3D elements, high-end animations, and modern UI components inspired by libraries like mvp-subha.me, shsfui, kibo-ui, and 21st.dev.

User Review Required
IMPORTANT

3D Performance: Integrating Three.js/React Three Fiber can be resource-heavy. We will optimize for performance, but the initial load might be slightly higher.
Design Direction: The theme will shift towards a "Cyber-Premium" aesthetic (Dark mode, deep purples/reds, neon accents, glassmorphism).
