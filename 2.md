*   **Visual Flex:** Display their rank badge next to their name in the Profile Settings.

## 🔥 Retention & Engagement Ideas

### 1. **Daily Login Streak Rewards**
Give users a small reward just for opening the app every day. Day 1 = 5 XP, Day 7 = a discount coupon, Day 30 = free diamond top-up. A flame streak counter (like Duolingo) on their profile makes it addictive. Users hate breaking streaks.

### 2. **"Mystery Box" Weekly Drop**
Every Monday, sell a limited "Mystery Box" for a fixed price (e.g., Rs. 50). It randomly gives either a panel, diamonds, or a "Golden Ticket" (big discount). The randomness creates excitement and a reason to visit weekly.

### 3. **Personalized "Agent Profile" Card**
Replace boring profile pages with a stylized Free Fire-style agent card showing their username, rank, total purchases, XP level, badges earned, and squad size. Let them share it as an image to TikTok/WhatsApp — free viral marketing for you.

### 4. **"First Blood" First-Purchase Bonus**
When a user completes their very first order, trigger a full-screen animation saying "FIRST BLOOD 🩸" with a bonus reward (extra XP or a small wallet credit). It makes the first purchase feel like an achievement, not just a transaction.

### 5. **Loyalty Rank System**
Give users a rank that upgrades as they spend more:

| Rank | Spend | Perk |
|---|---|---|
| Recruit | 0–1000 Rs | — |
| Soldier | 1000–5000 Rs | 30 discount |
| Elite | 5000–15000 Rs | 50 + priority delivery |
| Legend | 30000+ Rs | 80 + exclusive products |

Showing the progress bar to the next rank on their dashboard creates a constant pull to "just buy one more."
